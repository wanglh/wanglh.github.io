一、AI辅助学习

1. AI support in self‐regulated learning: A decade of technological evolution and meta‐analysis 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2026-03-12) 作者：Jun Xu, Yuying Luo, Chengliang Wang, Mengji Wang, Yonghe Wu 摘要： 关于AI在自我调节学习中支持作用的十年技术演变与元分析研究，总结了该领域的发展脉络和研究成果。

2. Evaluating the Effects of Personalised Learning on AI‐Assisted Design Performance, Creative Self‐Efficacy, and Engagement of High‐ and Low‐Performing College Design Students 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-03-12) 作者：Jiawei Wang, Jingru Zhang, Yuan Kang 摘要： 评估个性化学习对AI辅助设计表现、创造性自我效能感和参与度的影响，研究对象包括高表现和低表现的大学设计专业学生。

3. Evaluating the Impact of AI Tools on Academic Performance and Student Satisfaction 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-03-10) 作者：Zijing Wu 摘要： 评估AI工具对学术表现和学生满意度的影响。

4. Comparing the Impact of Individual‐ and Team‐Based ChatGPT Collaboration on High School Students’ Cognitive Competencies in Programming Education 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-03-10) 作者：Mahmood H. Hussein, Fatimah Husayn Amir Aldeeb, Nor Lisa Sulaiman, Monther M. Elaish 摘要： 比较个人与团队使用ChatGPT协作对高中生编程教育中认知能力的影响。

5. When Generative AI Meets Socratic Method: Investigating Programming Learning Dynamics Through Behaviours, Interaction Qualities and Perceptions 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-02-17) 作者：Dan Sun, Yi Zheng, Jie Xu, Zhanshan Yang 摘要： 研究生成式AI与苏格拉底方法结合时，通过行为、互动质量和感知来调查编程学习的动态过程。

6. Students’ use patterns of generative artificial intelligence during problem‐solving in an intelligent学习系统: Achievement goal orientation matters 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2026-02-26) 作者：Tingting Wang, Shan Li, Shasha Li, Jianhua Zhang, Jie Gao 摘要： 研究学生在智能学习系统中解决问题时使用生成式AI的模式，以及成就目标取向的作用。

7. Generative AI in work‐integrated learning: Supporting pre‐service teachers’ emotional labour and self‐management in Australian initial teacher education 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2026-01-22) 作者：Ngoc Nhu Nguyen (Ruby), Walter Barbieri 摘要： 研究生成式AI在工作整合学习中的作用，支持澳大利亚初始教师教育中准教师的情感劳动和自我管理。

8. Development and evaluation of artificial intelligence literacy training for teacher education students 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2026-01-27) 作者：Tam Hong Le, Luna Huynh, Belle Dang, Hiep‐Hung Pham, Nga Thi Viet Nguyen, Andy Nguyen 摘要： 开发并评估面向师范生的AI素养培训项目。

2. Promoting Interdisciplinary Learning With Generative AI Through Self‐Regulated Scaffolding 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-02-25) 作者：Qi Xia, Yiming Yang, Wenlang Wang, Hongbiao Yin 摘要： 通过自我调节支架促进生成式AI支持的跨学科学习。

3. Optimizing self‐regulated learning: A mixed‐methods study on GAI’s impact on undergraduate task策略和元认知 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2025-09-17) 作者：Ping Wang, Tianle Liu, Yingying Yang, Xingyu Xiang 摘要： 混合方法研究生成式AI对本科生任务策略和元认知的影响，优化自我调节学习。

3. A Study of AI‐Supported Cross‐Cultural Learning and Its Influence on Cross‐Cultural Understanding, Learning Behaviour and Writing Performance of Learners in Authentic Contexts 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-02-18) 作者：Wu‐Yuin Hwang, Thi‐Rin‐Gan Nguyen, Rustam Shadiev 摘要： 研究AI支持的跨文化学习及其对真实情境中学习者跨文化理解、学习行为和写作表现的影响。

4. Exploring collaborative learning in makerspaces through a complex systems lens: Aligning theory and method for deeper insights 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2025-11-25) 作者：Anders I. Mørch, Kristina T. Litherland 摘要： 从复杂系统视角探索创客空间中的协作学习，对齐理论和方法以获得更深入的洞察。

5. Sustained Idea Improvement: Validating a Conceptual Model for Knowledge Building in a Graduate Online Course 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-02-17) 作者：Huang‐Yao Hong, Mei‐Ju Chen, Chih‐Hsuan Chang 摘要： 验证研究生在线课程中知识构建的概念模型，实现持续的思想改进。

6. Applying a Think‐Pair‐Share (TPS) Structure to Asynchronous Online Discussions (AODs): A Mixed Methods Study 期刊：Journal of Computer Assisted Learning (2026-01-29) 作者：Tianxiao Yang, Jing Lei, Zhijuan Niu 摘要： 将思考-配对-分享（TPS）结构应用于异步在线讨论的混合方法研究。

7. Teachers’ professional agency in learning with AI: A case study of a generative AI‐based knowledge building learning companion for teachers 期刊：British Journal of Educational Technology (2025-09-02) 作者：Seng Chee Tan, Yee Yin Tan, Chew Lee Teo, Guangji Yuan 摘要： 教师在与AI学习中的专业能动性研究，基于生成式AI的知识构建学习同伴的案例研究。
